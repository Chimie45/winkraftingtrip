Our Story

When in Korea (WinK) was born from a passion for sharing the incredible adventures that South Korea has to offer. Founded by adventure enthusiasts who fell in love with Korea's diverse landscapes and rich culture, we specialize in creating unforgettable experiences that go beyond typical tourist attractions.

From heart-pounding white-water rafting in pristine mountain valleys to exhilarating ATV rides through scenic countryside, we curate adventures that showcase Korea's natural beauty while providing safe, professional, and thrilling experiences for adventurers of all levels.
