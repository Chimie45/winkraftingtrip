Experience the adrenaline rush of navigating Korea's pristine mountain rivers with our internationally certified rafting guides. Our summer rafting adventure takes place on the stunning Hantan River, renowned for its crystal-clear glacial waters and perfectly graded rapids.

🌊 Rafting Experience Details

Duration: 4 hours including comprehensive safety briefing
Difficulty: Class II-III rapids (Perfect for beginners to intermediates)
Group Size: 6-8 adventurers per professional-grade raft
Season: June-August (Optimal water levels and weather)

🏔️ The Hantan River Experience

Your rafting journey begins with a comprehensive safety briefing from our internationally certified guides. Learn proper paddling techniques, safety procedures, and river navigation before embarking on your aquatic adventure through some of Korea's most spectacular scenery.

The Hantan River offers the perfect combination of thrilling Class II-III rapids and serene floating sections where you can admire the breathtaking mountain vistas. During calmer moments, keep your eyes peeled for native wildlife including Korean water deer, mountain hawks, and the occasional river otter family!

🛡️ Premium Safety & Equipment

Your safety is our top priority. All participants receive professional-grade equipment including impact-rated helmets, Coast Guard approved life jackets, thermal wetsuits (temperature dependent), and carbon fiber paddles. Our rafts undergo daily safety inspections and meet international whitewater safety standards.
